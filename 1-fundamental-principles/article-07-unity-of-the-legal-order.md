# Article 7 â€“ Unity of the Legal Order

1. The laws of the Principality of Kaharagia form a single, coherent legal order.

2. All Codes shall be interpreted in harmony with one another and with the principles set forth in this Book.

3. In cases of doubt or apparent conflict, interpretation shall favour the preservation of legal coherence and fidelity to the Sovereign authority.
