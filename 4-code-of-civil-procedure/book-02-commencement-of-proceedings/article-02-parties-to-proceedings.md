# Article 2 â€“ Parties to Proceedings

1. Any person with legal capacity may be a party to civil proceedings.

2. Minors and persons under protection shall be represented by guardians or curators.

3. Juridical persons shall be represented by their legal representatives.

4. Multiple claimants or defendants may be joined where their rights or liabilities arise from the same facts or legal questions.

5. The court may order joinder or separation of parties in the interests of justice.
