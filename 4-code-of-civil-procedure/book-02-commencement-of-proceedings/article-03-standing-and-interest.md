# Article 3 â€“ Standing and Interest

1. A person may bring a claim only if they have a direct legal interest in the matter.

2. Associations may bring claims on behalf of their members where authorized by law or their statutes.

3. The court may permit intervention by persons with a legal interest in the outcome.

4. Public interest litigation may be brought where expressly authorized by law.
