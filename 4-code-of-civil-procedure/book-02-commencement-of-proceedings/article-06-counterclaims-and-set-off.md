# Article 6 â€“ Counterclaims and Set-Off

1. A defendant may assert a counterclaim arising from the same transaction or closely related matters.

2. A counterclaim shall be included in the statement of defense and shall comply with the requirements for a claim.

3. The claimant shall respond to any counterclaim within 20 days.

4. A defendant may raise set-off as a defense to reduce or extinguish the claim.
