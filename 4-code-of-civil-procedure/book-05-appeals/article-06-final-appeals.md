# Article 6 â€“ Final Appeals

1. A decision of the appellate division is final.

2. In exceptional cases involving matters of great public importance or manifest injustice, a party may petition The Sovereign for review.

3. The Sovereign may, in the exercise of the prerogative of justice, refer the matter for reconsideration or grant any relief deemed appropriate.
