# Article 4 â€“ Appellate Procedure

1. Appeals shall proceed by way of written submissions unless oral hearing is ordered.

2. The appellant shall file written submissions within 90 days of the notice of appeal.

3. The respondent shall file responding submissions within 30 days thereafter.

4. Reply submissions may be filed within 14 days if permitted.
