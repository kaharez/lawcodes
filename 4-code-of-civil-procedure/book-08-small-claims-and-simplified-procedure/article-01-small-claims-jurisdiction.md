# Article 1 â€“ Small Claims Jurisdiction

1. The court shall maintain a small claims division for claims not exceeding a prescribed monetary limit.

2. Small claims procedure is simplified and informal to facilitate access to justice.

3. Representation by lawyers is permitted but not required.

4. The court may give directions to ensure fairness.
