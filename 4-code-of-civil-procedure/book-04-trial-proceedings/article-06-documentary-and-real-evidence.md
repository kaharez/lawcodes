# Article 6 â€“ Documentary and Real Evidence

1. Documents and objects may be admitted in evidence if properly authenticated.

2. The court may admit evidence subject to subsequent proof of authenticity.

3. Business records are admissible if accompanied by an affidavit of their keeper.

4. The court may view sites or objects relevant to the case.
