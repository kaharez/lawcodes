# Article 5 â€“ Examination of Witnesses

1. Witnesses shall be examined orally in court, unless otherwise permitted.

2. Examination shall proceed by:
   (a) examination-in-chief by the party calling the witness;
   (b) cross-examination by other parties;
   (c) re-examination (limited to matters arising from cross-examination).

3. The court may put questions to any witness.

4. Leading questions are generally not permitted in examination-in-chief.
