# Article 1 â€“ Notice of Trial

1. The court shall fix a trial date after the pre-trial conference.

2. Notice of trial shall be given to all parties at least 30 days in advance.

3. A party may apply for adjournment only on showing good cause.

4. Failure to appear at trial may result in dismissal or judgment in absence.
