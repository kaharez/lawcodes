# Article 4 â€“ Declaratory Relief

1. The court may grant declaratory relief where there is a real dispute about legal rights, even if no other remedy is sought.

2. Declarations bind the parties and state the legal position.

3. Declaratory relief is discretionary and will not be granted for hypothetical questions.
