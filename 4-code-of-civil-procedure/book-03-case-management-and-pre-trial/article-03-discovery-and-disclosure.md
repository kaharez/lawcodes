# Article 3 â€“ Discovery and Disclosure

1. Parties shall disclose to each other all documents relevant to the matters in dispute.

2. Disclosure shall be made within 60 days of the close of pleadings, unless otherwise ordered.

3. A party may request production of specific documents in the possession of another party.

4. Privileged documents need not be disclosed but shall be listed with the basis for privilege claimed.

5. Failure to disclose may result in sanctions, including exclusion of evidence.
