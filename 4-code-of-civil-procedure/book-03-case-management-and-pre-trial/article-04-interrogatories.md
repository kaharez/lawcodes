# Article 4 â€“ Interrogatories

1. A party may serve written questions (interrogatories) on another party.

2. Interrogatories shall be limited to matters relevant to the case.

3. Answers shall be provided within 30 days and shall be sworn or affirmed.

4. The court may limit interrogatories if they are excessive or burdensome.
