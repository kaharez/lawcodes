# Article 8 â€“ Settlement and Alternative Dispute Resolution

1. The court shall encourage settlement at all stages of the proceeding.

2. The court may order parties to attend mediation or other alternative dispute resolution.

3. Settlement discussions are confidential and without prejudice.

4. If parties reach settlement, they shall file a consent order or notice of discontinuance.

5. The court may make an order giving effect to the settlement.
