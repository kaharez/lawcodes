# Article 7 â€“ Time Limits and Computation

1. Time limits prescribed by this Code or by court order shall be computed in accordance with Fundamental Principles, Article 4.

2. The court may extend time limits on application showing good cause.

3. Failure to comply with time limits may result in dismissal of claims or defenses, unless the court grants relief.
