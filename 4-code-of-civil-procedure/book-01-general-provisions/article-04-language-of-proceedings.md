# Article 4 â€“ Language of Proceedings

1. Proceedings shall be conducted in English, the official language of the courts.

2. Documents in other languages shall be accompanied by certified translations.

3. The court may permit oral interpretation for parties or witnesses who do not understand English.

4. Judgments and orders shall be issued in English.
