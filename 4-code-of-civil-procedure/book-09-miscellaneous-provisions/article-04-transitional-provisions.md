# Article 4 â€“ Transitional Provisions

1. This Code applies to all proceedings commenced after its entry into force.

2. Proceedings commenced before entry into force shall continue under the previous procedure, unless the court orders otherwise.

3. Where no previous procedure existed, this Code applies to all pending matters.
