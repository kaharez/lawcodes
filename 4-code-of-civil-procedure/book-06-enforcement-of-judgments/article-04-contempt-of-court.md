# Article 4 â€“ Contempt of Court

1. Willful failure to comply with a court order constitutes contempt of court.

2. Contempt may be civil (to compel compliance) or criminal (to punish disobedience).

3. Penalties for contempt include fines or detention until compliance.

4. The court may purge contempt upon compliance with the order.
