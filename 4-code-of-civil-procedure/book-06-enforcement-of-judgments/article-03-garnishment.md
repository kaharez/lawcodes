# Article 3 â€“ Garnishment

1. The court may order a third party owing money to the judgment debtor to pay directly to the judgment creditor.

2. Garnishment applies to wages, bank accounts, and other debts.

3. A portion of wages is exempt to ensure the debtor's subsistence.

4. The garnishee must file a statement showing amounts owed and paid.
