# Article 3 â€“ Tax Evasion and Fiscal Fraud

1. Tax evasion is the deliberate and dishonest failure to declare income, assets, or transactions, or the making of false declarations, for the purpose of avoiding or reducing tax obligations owed to the State or to any jurisdiction with which the State has fiscal cooperation agreements.

2. Tax evasion and fiscal fraud include:
    (a) failing to declare taxable income, profits, or gains;
    (b) making false statements or providing false information in tax returns or declarations;
    (c) claiming false deductions, credits, or exemptions;
    (d) concealing assets or income in undeclared accounts or through offshore structures;
    (e) issuing or using false invoices or receipts to evade tax;
    (f) operating businesses or engaging in transactions without proper registration or declaration;
    (g) facilitating, enabling, or advising tax evasion by others; or
    (h) any other deliberate and dishonest conduct for the purpose of evading fiscal obligations.

3. Tax evasion is punished under Penalty Band D.

4. The penalty is aggravated to Penalty Band E where:
    (a) the amount of tax evaded is very substantial;
    (b) the evasion is systematic, sophisticated, or involves cross-border schemes;
    (c) the evasion involves use of shell companies, nominee structures, or complex financial arrangements;
    (d) the evasion is committed by persons in professional or fiduciary positions, including accountants, tax advisors, or financial professionals;
    (e) the evasion involves multiple jurisdictions or exploitation of tax treaties; or
    (f) the offender has previous convictions for tax evasion or fiscal fraud.

5. Juridical persons may be held liable for tax evasion committed by their directors, officers, employees, or agents.

6. The court may order payment of evaded taxes, penalties, interest, and confiscation of proceeds of tax evasion.

7. This Article applies to evasion of taxes, duties, levies, or other fiscal obligations owed to the State of Kaharagia or to jurisdictions with which Kaharagia has agreements for fiscal cooperation and mutual assistance.
