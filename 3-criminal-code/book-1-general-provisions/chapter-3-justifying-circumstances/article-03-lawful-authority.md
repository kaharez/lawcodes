# Article 3 â€“ Lawful Authority

1. An act does not constitute an offence where it is performed in the execution of a lawful order issued by a competent authority of the State of Kaharagia.

2. Orders that are manifestly unlawful shall not justify compliance.

3. A subordinate who refuses to execute a manifestly unlawful order shall incur no criminal liability.
