# Article 3 â€“ Suspension And Modification Of Penalties

1. The court may suspend the execution of a penalty, in whole or in part, where:
    (a) the convicted person demonstrates sincere remorse;
    (b) restitution has been made; or
    (c) suspension is necessary to prevent undue hardship.

2. The court may modify a penalty upon:
    (a) material change in circumstances;
    (b) discovery of new evidence relevant to sentencing;
    (c) agreement of the parties in restorative justice processes approved by the court.

3. Suspension or modification of penalties shall not apply to penalties expressly declared mandatory by this Code or by Sovereign Decree.
