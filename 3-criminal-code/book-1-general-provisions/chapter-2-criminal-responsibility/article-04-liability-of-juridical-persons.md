# Article 4 â€“ Liability of Juridical Persons

1. Juridical persons under the law of the State of Kaharagia may be held criminally liable for offences committed:
    (a) by their organs or authorised representatives;
    (b) through acts carried out in their name or interest; or
    (c) through serious organisational deficiencies that enable or permit the offence.

2. Criminal liability of a juridical person does not preclude the criminal liability of natural persons involved.

3. Penalties applicable to juridical persons include:
    (a) fines and restitution;
    (b) suspension of registration or legal personality;
    (c) disqualification from contracts or commissions with the State;
    (d) dissolution in cases of extreme gravity.
