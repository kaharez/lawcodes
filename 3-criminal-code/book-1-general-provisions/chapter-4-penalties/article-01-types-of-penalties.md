# Article 1 â€“ Types of Penalties

1. Penalties imposed under this Code include:
    (a) reprimand;
    (b) public censure, including publication of the judgment;
    (c) fine or day-fine;
    (d) restitution or compensation to victims;
    (e) suspension or revocation of Verified Kaharagian Status;
    (f) disqualification from public office, commissions, honours, or regulated activities;
    (g) confiscation of instruments, proceeds, or digital assets connected with the offence;
    (h) community service or service by Sovereign commission;
    (i) custodial penalty, enforceable only through recognised foreign cooperation;
    (j) dissolution of juridical persons in cases of extreme gravity.

2. Accessory penalties may accompany principal penalties where the nature of the offence so requires.

3. Penalties shall be imposed in accordance with the principles of necessity, proportionality, and respect for human dignity.
