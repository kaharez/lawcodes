# Article 2 – Penalty Bands

1. Penalties are grouped into bands, which define the general severity applicable to each offence:
    a. Band A – reprimand, public censure, and minor fines;
    b. Band B – fines, day-fines, and restitution;
    c. Band C – fines combined with suspension of Verified Status for a period of 1 to 5 years;
    d. Band D – significant fines, restitution, disqualification, and confiscation;
    e. Band E – all penalties in Band D, with the addition of custodial penalties where enforceable.

2. The Special Part of this Code assigns each offence to the applicable penalty band.

3. The court may not exceed the maximum of the band unless expressly authorised by law or Sovereign Decree.
