# Article 4 â€“ Unauthorised Conferment of Titles or Honours

1. Any person who grants, sells, offers, or purports to confer Kaharagian honours, titles, orders, decorations, or ranks without Sovereign authority commits an offence.

2. This offence includes:
    (a) creating false documents or claims regarding noble titles;
    (b) issuing unauthorised certificates, letters patent, or registrations;
    (c) soliciting or accepting payment in exchange for appointments or honours;
    (d) representing a foreign title or dignity as Kaharagian without lawful registration.

3. This offence is punishable within Penalty Band C, and may warrant Penalty Band D where the act is part of a fraudulent or commercial scheme.
