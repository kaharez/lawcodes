# Article 3 â€“ Espionage

1. Any person commits espionage who intentionally obtains, transmits, or communicates information designated confidential, classified, or sensitive by the State of Kaharagia for the benefit of a foreign power, group, or individual.

2. Espionage includes:
    (a) infiltration or compromise of State systems, networks, or registers;
    (b) recruitment or direction of agents to acquire sensitive information;
    (c) surveillance or monitoring of State officials or systems on behalf of a foreign entity.

3. Espionage is punishable within Penalty Band D, or Band E where the act endangers State security or the Kaharagian community.

4. Attempted espionage is punishable as if the offence had been completed.
