# Article 1 â€“ False Claim of Nationality or Status

1. Any person who knowingly and falsely claims to be a Kaharagian national or to possess Verified Kaharagian Status commits an offence.

2. This offence includes:
    (a) submitting false information in an application for nationality or Verified Status;
    (b) presenting oneself as a citizen or Verified Kaharagian to obtain rights, services, or recognition;
    (c) using digital means to simulate or fabricate Verified Status.

3. This offence is punishable within Penalty Band C, or Penalty Band D where the false claim is made to deceive State authorities or to obtain legal privileges.
