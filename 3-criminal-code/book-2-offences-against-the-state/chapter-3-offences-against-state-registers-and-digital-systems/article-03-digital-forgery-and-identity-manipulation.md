# Article 3 â€“ Digital Forgery and Identity Manipulation

1. Any person who creates, modifies, or uses a digital identifier, token, credential, or signature purporting to be issued by the State, without lawful authority, commits an offence.

2. Identity manipulation includes:
    a. forging Verified Kaharagian Status;
    b. altering digital identity certificates;
    c. generating false identity codes or verification QR/barcode assets;
    d. impersonating a citizen or official through digital means.

3. This offence is punishable within Penalty Band D, or Penalty Band E where it affects nationality, identification, or the integrity of State verification systems.
