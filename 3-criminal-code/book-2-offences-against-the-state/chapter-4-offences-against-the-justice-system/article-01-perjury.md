# Article 1 â€“ Perjury

1. Any person who, in a judicial or official proceeding, intentionally makes a false statement under oath or solemn affirmation commits the offence of perjury.

2. Perjury includes:
    a. knowingly providing false evidence;
    b. concealing material facts when under a duty to disclose;
    c. submitting fabricated documents or digital evidence.

3. Perjury is punishable within Penalty Band C, or Penalty Band D where it affects the outcome of a criminal case or undermines the integrity of the High Court.
