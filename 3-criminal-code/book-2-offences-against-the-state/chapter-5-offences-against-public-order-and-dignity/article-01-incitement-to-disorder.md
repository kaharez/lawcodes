# Article 1 â€“ Incitement to Disorder

1. Any person who intentionally incites, encourages, or organises conduct that disturbs public order or undermines the peace of the Kaharagian community commits an offence.

2. Incitement includes:
    (a) provoking collective unrest;
    (b) urging disobedience to lawful State authority;
    (c) instigating digital campaigns designed to disrupt public communications or State functions.

3. This offence is punishable within Penalty Band C, or Penalty Band D where the act results in actual disorder or threatens the integrity of State institutions.
