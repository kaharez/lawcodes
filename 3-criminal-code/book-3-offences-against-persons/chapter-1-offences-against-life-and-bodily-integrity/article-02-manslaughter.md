# Article 2 â€“ Manslaughter

1. Any person who causes the death of another through negligence or recklessness commits manslaughter.

2. Manslaughter is punishable within Penalty Band D, or Penalty Band C where the offender acted without gross negligence.

3. The court shall consider the nature of the negligence, the foreseeability of harm, and the conduct of the offender after the incident.
