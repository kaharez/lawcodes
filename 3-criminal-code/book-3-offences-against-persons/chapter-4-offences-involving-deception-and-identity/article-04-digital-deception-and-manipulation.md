# Article 4 â€“ Digital Deception and Manipulation

1. Any person who intentionally uses digital tools to deceive, mislead, or manipulate another person commits an offence.

2. Digital deception includes:
    a. creating or distributing manipulated images, recordings, or digital materials intending to mislead;
    b. generating deepfakes to impersonate a person or fabricate events;
    c. using bots, scripts, or automation to simulate human identity or communication;
    d. falsifying metadata, timestamps, or authenticity markers.

3. This offence is punishable within Penalty Band C, or Penalty Band D where the act causes significant harm, facilitates fraud, or affects legal rights.
