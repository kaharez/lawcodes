# Article 1 â€“ Unlawful Detention

1. Any person who unlawfully confines, restrains, or deprives another of their liberty commits the offence of unlawful detention.

2. This offence is punishable within Penalty Band C, or Penalty Band D where the detention is prolonged, violent, or committed for financial gain.
