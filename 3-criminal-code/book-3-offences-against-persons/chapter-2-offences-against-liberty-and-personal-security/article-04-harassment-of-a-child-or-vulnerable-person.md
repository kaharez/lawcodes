# Article 4 â€“ Harassment of a Child or Vulnerable Person

1. Any person who targets, harasses, coerces, or abuses a child or vulnerable person commits an offence.

2. This offence is punishable within Penalty Band C, or Penalty Band D where the conduct is repeated or harmful.

3. The court shall consider any power imbalance, dependency, or manipulation as aggravating factors.
