# Article 6 â€“ Transitional and Exceptional Provisions

1. Persons recognised as Kaharagian nationals prior to the entry into force of this Code shall be entered in the National Register without further formality, on the basis of existing decrees or records.

2. The Sovereign may, by decree, admit or exclude individuals from nationality in circumstances not expressly provided for in this Code, where such action is deemed necessary in the interest of the state.``

3. In matters not expressly covered by this Title, questions relating to nationality shall be resolved by sovereign decree, taking into account the principles set forth in this Code.
