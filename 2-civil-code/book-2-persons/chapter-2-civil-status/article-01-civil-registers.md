# Article 1 â€“ Civil Registers

1. The Royal Chancellery shall maintain official civil registers, distinct from but coordinated with the Register of Kaharagian Nationals.

2. These registers shall record:
    (a) births;
    (b)  marriages and civil unions;
    (c)  adoptions;
    (d) deaths;
    (e) other civil acts expressly provided for by law or decree.

3. The registers shall be maintained primarily in digital form, with appropriate safeguards for authenticity and preservation.

4. Entries shall be made promptly following the event and upon presentation of the required documentation or decree.
