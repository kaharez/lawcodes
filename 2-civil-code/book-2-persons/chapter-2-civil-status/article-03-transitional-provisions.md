# Article 3 – Transitional Provisions

1. Civil acts lawfully recorded prior to the entry into force of this Code shall remain valid.
2. The Royal Chancellery shall ensure that pre‑existing records are transcribed or referenced in the new digital registers, preserving continuity of legal status.
3. In the event of conflict between older records and this Code, the Sovereign shall resolve the matter by decree, taking into account the principles of this Code and the interests of those concerned.
