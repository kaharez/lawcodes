# Article 4 â€“ Protection of Rights

1. The civil rights guaranteed under this Title are protected by the Sovereign and shall be administered and safeguarded by the Royal Chancellery.
2. Any person alleging infringement of their civil rights may petition the Sovereign, through the Chancellery, for redress, correction of records, or issuance of a decree to restore their lawful status.
3. Procedures for the protection and enforcement of civil rights may be further regulated by sovereign decree or by instruments issued under the authority of the Chancellery.
