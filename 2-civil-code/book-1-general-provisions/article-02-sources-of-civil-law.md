# Article 2 â€“ Sources of Civil Law

1. The sources of civil law in Kaharagia are, in order of precedence:
   (a) the Fundamental Principles;
   (b) Sovereign decrees on matters of prerogative;
   (c) this Civil Code and other codified law;
   (d) regulations and ordinances;
   (e) custom, where recognized by law or jurisprudence.

2. In the absence of express legal provision, the court shall decide according to:
   (a) analogy with existing provisions;
   (b) general principles of law;
   (c) natural justice and equity.

3. The court shall always strive to give effect to the underlying purposes and principles of the law.
