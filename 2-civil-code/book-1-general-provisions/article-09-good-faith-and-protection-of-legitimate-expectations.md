# Article 9 â€“ Good Faith and Protection of Legitimate Expectations

1. All persons must act in good faith in the exercise of their civil rights and the performance of their civil obligations.

2. Good faith is presumed; bad faith must be proved.

3. A person who has legitimately relied on another's representation or conduct is entitled to protection of their reasonable expectations.

4. No person may resile from their own prior acts or representations to the detriment of another who has relied thereon in good faith.

5. Contractual and legal relationships shall be performed with honesty, fairness, and regard for the reasonable interests of the other party.
