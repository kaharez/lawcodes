# Article 10 â€“ Prescription and Limitation

1. Rights and actions may be extinguished or acquired by prescription in accordance with the provisions of this Code.

2. Prescription runs in favor of all persons, including those without capacity, unless specifically suspended or interrupted by law.

3. Prescription may not be renounced in advance.

4. The court may not apply prescription of its own motion; it must be invoked by the interested party.

5. Specific provisions on prescription periods are found throughout this Code in relation to particular rights and actions.
