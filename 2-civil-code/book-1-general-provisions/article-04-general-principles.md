# Article 4 â€“ General Principles

1. All civil rights and obligations shall be exercised and performed in good faith.

2. The abuse of rights is prohibited. A right is abused when exercised:
   a. solely to harm another;
   b. in a manner grossly disproportionate to legitimate interests;
   c. contrary to its social or economic purpose.

3. Parties to civil relationships owe each other duties of:
   a. fairness and fair dealing;
   b. reasonable cooperation;
   c. respect for the legitimate interests of others.

4. In case of doubt, the law favors:
   a. liberty over restraint;
   b. protection of the weak and vulnerable;
   c. maintenance of legal relationships over their dissolution.
