# Article 8 â€“ Computation of Time

1. Time periods prescribed by this Code or by legal act are computed as follows:
   (a) a period expressed in days shall run from midnight to midnight;
   (b) the day on which the period begins to run is not counted;
   (c) the period expires at midnight on the last day.

2. If the last day falls on a non-business day, the period is extended to the next business day.

3. Periods expressed in months or years are computed:
   (a) from the same day of the month or year;
   (b) if no corresponding day exists, until the last day of the month.

4. References to "working days" exclude weekends and official public holidays.

5. These rules apply unless otherwise specified in particular provisions.
