# Article 2 â€“ Wards of the Crown

1. A minor child who has no living parents or legal guardians shall become a Ward of the Crown.
2. Upon becoming a Ward of the Crown, the child shall automatically acquire Kaharagian nationality if not already a national.
3. The Royal Chancellery shall act as guardian until a permanent guardian is appointed or adoption is effected.
4. The Sovereign may, by decree, designate a suitable guardian or approve placement with a family, institution, or other arrangement.
