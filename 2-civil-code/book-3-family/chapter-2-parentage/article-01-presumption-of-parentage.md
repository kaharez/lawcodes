# Article 1 â€“ Presumption of Parentage

1. A child born during a marriage, or within three hundred days after its dissolution, is presumed to be the child of both spouses.
2. This presumption may be rebutted only by decree or adjudication in accordance with this Code.
3. In cases where spouses are of the same sex, parentage of a child conceived or adopted during the marriage shall be determined by declaration to the Chancellery or by decree.
