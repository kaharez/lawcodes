# Article 4 â€“ Duties of Parents Toward Children

1. Parents owe their children care, support, education, and guidance, and shall respect their dignity and personal development.
2. Parents shall jointly exercise parental authority unless otherwise determined by decree or adjudication.
3. The Royal Chancellery may intervene, by decree or appropriate measure, to safeguard the welfare of the child where parental duties are gravely neglected or contested.
