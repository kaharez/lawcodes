# Article 2 â€“ Celebration and Registration of Marriage

1. Marriage shall be celebrated before a civil registrar designated by the Royal Chancellery or another person specially commissioned by the Sovereign.
2. Religious or ceremonial marriages may be performed, but they do not produce legal effect unless recorded in the civil registers in accordance with this Code.
3. The marriage shall be entered in the civil registers maintained by the Chancellery, and a certificate of marriage shall be issued to the spouses.
4. The marriage may be announced in the Royal Kaharagian Gazette where ceremonial or public notice is appropriate.
