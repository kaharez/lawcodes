# Article 4 â€“ Dissolution of Marriage

1. Marriage is dissolved:
    (a)  by the death of either spouse;
    (b) by a decree of annulment where the marriage was void or voidable under this Code;
    (c) by sovereign decree of dissolution, granted upon petition for grave cause recognised by law or decree.
2. The decree or certificate of dissolution shall be recorded in the civil registers and, where appropriate, published in the Royal Kaharagian Gazette.
