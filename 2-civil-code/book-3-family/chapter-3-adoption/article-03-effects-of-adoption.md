# Article 3 â€“ Effects of Adoption

1. The adopted person shall bear the family name of the adopter(s), unless otherwise determined by decree.
2. A minor adopted by a Kaharagian national shall automatically acquire Kaharagian nationality upon the issuance of the decree of adoption.
3. Adoption of an adult does not confer nationality unless expressly provided in the decree.
4. The adoption may not be revoked except by sovereign decree for grave cause, including fraud or abuse.
