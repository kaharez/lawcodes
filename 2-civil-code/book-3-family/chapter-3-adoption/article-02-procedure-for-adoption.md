# Article 2 â€“ Procedure for Adoption

1. Adoption shall be effected by petition to the Royal Chancellery and confirmed by sovereign decree.
2. The Chancellery shall examine the suitability of the adopter(s) and the best interests of the child, and present the petition for formal decree.
3. Upon issuance of the decree, the adoption shall be entered in the civil registers and a certificate of adoption issued to the adoptive family.
