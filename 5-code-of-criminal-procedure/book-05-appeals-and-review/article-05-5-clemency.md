# Article 5 â€“ Clemency

1. The Sovereign may exercise the prerogative of mercy as provided in the Criminal Code, Book 1, Chapter 6, Article 4.

2. Petitions for clemency shall be addressed to The Sovereign.

3. The grant of clemency is a sovereign prerogative and not subject to judicial review.
