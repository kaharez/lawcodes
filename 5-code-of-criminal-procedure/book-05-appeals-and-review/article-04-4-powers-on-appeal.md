# Article 4 â€“ Powers on Appeal

1. The appellate court may:
   a. dismiss the appeal;
   b. allow the appeal and acquit;
   c. order a new trial;
   d. substitute a different verdict or sentence;
   e. make any order the trial court could have made.

2. The onus is on the appellant to show error.
