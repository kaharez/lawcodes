# Article 5 â€“ Order of Trial

1. Trial proceeds in this order:
   (a) reading of the charge;
   (b) plea;
   (c) prosecution opening;
   (d) prosecution evidence;
   (e) defense case;
   (f) closing arguments;
   (g) judgment.

2. The court may vary the order in the interests of justice.
