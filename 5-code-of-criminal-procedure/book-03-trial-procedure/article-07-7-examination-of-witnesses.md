# Article 7 â€“ Examination of Witnesses

1. Witnesses shall testify orally under oath or affirmation.

2. The party calling a witness examines first (examination-in-chief).

3. Other parties may cross-examine.

4. Re-examination is permitted on matters arising from cross-examination.

5. The court may question witnesses.
