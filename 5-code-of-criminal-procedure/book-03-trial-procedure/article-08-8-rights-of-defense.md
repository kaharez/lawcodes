# Article 8 â€“ Rights of Defense

1. The accused may:
   (a) testify or remain silent;
   (b) call witnesses;
   (c) cross-examine prosecution witnesses;
   (d) present evidence;
   (e) make submissions.

2. No adverse inference shall be drawn from silence.
