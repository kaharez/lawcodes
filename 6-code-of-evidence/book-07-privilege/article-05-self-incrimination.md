# Article 5 â€“ Self-Incrimination

1. No person shall be compelled to give evidence that may incriminate them in criminal proceedings.

2. This privilege does not apply to compelled testimony that is granted immunity.

3. A witness claiming privilege must establish a real and appreciable risk of incrimination.
