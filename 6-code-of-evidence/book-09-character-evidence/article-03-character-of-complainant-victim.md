# Article 3 â€“ Character of Complainant/Victim

1. The character of complainants or victims is generally inadmissible.

2. Sexual history of complainants in sexual offence cases is inadmissible except where specifically relevant and admitted by the court after hearing.

3. Such evidence requires application showing relevance and balancing probative value against prejudice.
