# Article 1 â€“ Hearsay Rule

1. Hearsay is an out-of-court statement tendered to prove the truth of its contents.

2. Hearsay is generally inadmissible.

3. Hearsay may be admitted if it falls within an exception or if its admission is necessary and reliable.
