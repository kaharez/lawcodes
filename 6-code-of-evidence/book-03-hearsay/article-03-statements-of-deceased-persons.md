# Article 3 â€“ Statements of Deceased Persons

1. Statements of deceased persons may be admitted if:
   (a) made against the declarant's interest;
   (b) concerning the declarant's state of mind or bodily condition;
   (c) concerning family history;
   (d) dying declarations (in homicide cases, where death is imminent).

2. The statement must be shown to be reliable.
