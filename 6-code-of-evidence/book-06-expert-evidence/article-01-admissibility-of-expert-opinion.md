# Article 1 â€“ Admissibility of Expert Opinion

1. Expert opinion is admissible on matters requiring specialized knowledge beyond the court's experience.

2. The expert must be qualified by training, study, or experience.

3. The opinion must be based on reliable methods and sufficient data.
